Awards and Accolades

Throughout 75+ years of existence, the Epsilon Zeta chapter has been recognized by a number of local organizations and APO nationals for achievement to our community, campus, and country. Our most notable achievement is winning the Dean Arno Nowotny National Service Award 6x. Below are our other notable achievements.

PreviousNext

Highlights

  • H. Roe Bartle Chapter Award

    1976, 1977, 1978, 1979, 1980

    From: APO National Office
    Purpose: Outstanding achievement in chapter administration and program.

  • Arno Nowotny National Service Award

    1980

    From: APO National Office
    Purpose: Conducting the most significant service program during 1978-1980.

  • H. Roe Bartle Chapter Award

    1981, 1983, 1984, 1986, 1999 

    From: APO National Office
    Purpose: Outstanding achievement in chapter administration and program.

  • City of Troy Proclamation

    1981

    From: City of Troy, Mayor's Office
    Purpose: Recognize EZ for continued community impact and winning their first nowotny. Declaration of March 30, 1981 as Alpha Phi Omega Day in the City of Troy.

  • RPI Community Service Award and Proclamation

    1982 

    From: RPI Board of Trustees
    Purpose: Award EZ's continued community engagement and community service.

  • Arno Nowotny National Service Award

    1984

    From: APO National Office
    Purpose: Conducting the most significant service program during 1982-1984.

  • M R Disborough Scouting Service Award

    1984 

    From: APO National Office
    Purpose: Exceptional service to Scouting as an integral part of the overall service program.

  • City of Troy Proclamation

    1985

    From: City of Troy, Mayor's Office
    Purpose: Recognize EZ for community impact and winning their second nowotny and the M R Disborough Scouting Service Award in 1984. Declaration of March 5, 1985 as Epsilon Zeta Day in the City of Troy.

  • City of Troy Proclamation

    1987 

    From: City of Troy, Mayor's Office
    Purpose: Recognize EZ for community impact and to congratulate the chapter on their 40th year. Declaration of March 28, 1987 as Alpha Phi Omega Day in the City of Troy.

  • Service Award

    1997

    From: APO Region 2
    Purpose: Recognize EZ for For outstanding service in the community during 1997.

  • City of Troy Proclamation

    1997 

    From: City of Troy, Mayor's Office
    Purpose: Recognize EZ for community impact and to congratulate the chapter on their 50th year. Declaration of May 11, 1997 as Alpha Phi Omega Day in the City of Troy.

  • Arno Nowotny National Service Award

    1998, 2004

    From: APO National Office
    Purpose: Conducting the most significant service program.

  • Josiah Frank Historian's Award

    2004

    From: APO National Office
    Purpose: Recognize EZ For excellence in compiling and maintaining chapter history, 2002-2004.

    Special thanks to Doctor Alyssa Pasquale for all their work done in compiling records! 

  • Rensselaer Union Volunteer Recognition Award

    2004

    From: Rensselaer Union
    Purpose: Outstanding service to the local and/or national community by a club or organization at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.

  • Arno Nowotny National Service Award

    2010, 2023 

    From: APO National Office
    Purpose: Conducting the most significant service program.

  • Josiah Frank Historian's Award

    2023

    From: APO National Office
    Purpose: Recognize EZ For excellence in compiling and maintaining chapter history, 2022-2023.
